## Sprint Packet Sections

Every sprint packet must include:

1. **Goal** — What success looks like
2. **Definition of Done** — Specific completion criteria
3. **In-Scope Files/Paths** — What can be touched
4. **Contract References** — Endpoints/schemas
5. **Acceptance Criteria** — Testable requirements (Gherkin format)
6. **Technical Requirements** — Security, logging, performance
7. **Dependencies** — External blockers
8. **Risks** — Identified with mitigations
9. **Ceremonies** — Sprint meeting schedule
10. **Sign-Off** — Pre and post-sprint approvals
